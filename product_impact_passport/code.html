Our Origins

Born from a need for silence and substance.

CoLiv was founded in 2021 by Elena Vance, following a decade in fast-fashion that left her disillusioned. She sought to create a brand that didn't just sell products, but offered a "sanctuary"—a deliberate pause in a hyper-accelerated world.

Our mission is radical longevity. We believe beauty is a byproduct of ethical integrity and material honesty. Every piece we create is designed to be lived in, repaired, and eventually returned to the earth.
