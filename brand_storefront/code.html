Our Mission

Radical transparency in textiles.

Aether Linen was born out of a desire to decouple luxury from environmental degradation. Our founder, navigating the opaque supply chains of high fashion, realized the true cost of 'premium' fabrics.

We work exclusively with GOTS-certified organic flax, grown regeneratively. Our manufacturing process utilizes a closed-loop water system, ensuring zero toxic runoff into local waterways.
